Classify Movies Posters

When looking at a movie poster you never saw before, in a train station for example, you can generally get a sense of the kind of movie ist is (big colorful fonts signal comedy, faded colors a more serious tone, etc.) This made me realize it should be possible to train a small CNN model to detect this.

Are rappers Gemini?

I was arguing with somebody about the legitimacy of astrology. After some back-and-forth, the person stated that since "most rappers were Gemini", that would indicate some level of correlation between astrological sign and areas of interest.

I wanted to verify that claim.

Since it was a falsifiable statement, this kindled my curiosity, and I took a couple of hours to write a small script that would fetch all rappers on Wikipedia along their date of birth. Quite unsurprisingly, one would find no remarkable or significant correlation between the musical genre and Gemini.

This may all seems a little bit odd and out of place, but I would argue it is important to develop the tools of critical thinking and debunk quackery everywhere it's found.
